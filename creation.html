Stimulate Creative Thoughts

Chatbots can contribute to the ideation process by offering suggestions and honing concepts. It can serve as an initial stepping stone for exploration, motivating users to expand upon its generated content and infuse it with their own distinctive style.
Also, by providing immediate access to vast amounts of information, it can fuel the emergence of fresh ideas and expand the horizons of knowledge. It can serve as a catalyst during brainstorming sessions, assisting individuals in exploring diverse perspectives and unearthing novel insights.


Problems

There are some who argue that AI models like ChatGPT could hinder human creativity by supplanting the need for original thought. They express concerns that people might excessively rely on AI-generated content, thereby diminishing their own imaginative capabilities.


While ChatGPT creation is developing rapidly, it also brings some moral and ethical dilemmas. Among them, there are mainly the following aspects: Who should be responsible for its creation?
While the outputs of ChatGPT can simulate, predict, and showcase the human mind in a more realistic manner, they cannot entirely escape the influence of the designers and the training data underlying its algorithms. Consequently, it becomes challenging to define the ownership, causality, and accountability of ChatGPT creations within this context. Additionally, the absence of provisions concerning the subject of creation and the designer makes it difficult to clearly establish the ownership of intellectual property rights in these creations.
