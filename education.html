Education

"The current data shows that the most significant number of chatbot users is in the 18-29 age group, which means that the impact of chatAI on education is bound to be huge."



Create New Learning Environment

By creating a new virtual learning and communication environment, Chatbot can provide personalized assistance for college students, making their learning more accessible and flexible. This is conducive to students' autonomous, targeted thinking outside the classroom.


Specifically, Chatbot can serve as a peer mentor to provide personalized assistance in college students' language learning, simulating real human conversation, which allows students to engage in personalized learning and communication in a virtual environment. Many students described ChatGPT as a peer tutor that provided great support,which was more accessible than their teachers.


Access Learning Resources

Chatbot can help students access more high-quality learning resources by providing relevant links and reference materials. Scholars believe that Chatbot has a compelling ability to search and organize information, which can provide more abundant answers to students' questions and allow them to use natural language to find the learning resources they need. Undoubtedly, this can broaden students' knowledge and improve their learning efficiency, helping them engage in independent and targeted thinking outside the classroom.


Narrow the Educational Inequality

Chatbot also helps to address the social issue of educational inequality and bridge the digital divide between students from different socioeconomic backgrounds.By harnessing the power of artificial intelligence, Chatbot can provide all students with opportunities for high-quality education, helping to create a more level playing field regardless of their socioeconomic status. This can be achieved by developing affordable AI education tools and platforms that can be accessed on various devices, including low-cost smartphones and tablets.

Thus, integrating ChatGPT into education can fundamentally change our educational paradigm, ultimately improving academic outcomes and providing a more equitable and engaging learning experience.


Problems

Chatbots allow students to use the data it contains directly, which may lead to excessive reliance on it, resulting in lazy thinking, thereby losing the ability to think actively and independently.

Using Chatbot could make young students complacent, leading to a lack of essential skills in evaluating information.OpenAI also acknowledges that Chatbot sometimes provides seemingly reasonable but incorrect or absurd answers. Chatbot cannot connect to the active internet or retrieve any information from social media, and its dataset is limited, making it possible to generate incorrect information.

What needs to be paid attention to even more is that artificial intelligence is not yet sufficiently developed in terms of ethics and morality.Users' data privacy cannot be guaranteed. For example, in order to obtain answers from Chatbot,teachers and students need to input a large amount of related data and information. During the process of Chatbot recognising the data and providing satisfactory responses to users, the stored data may be leaked.
